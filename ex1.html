Exploring the Wonders of Space

The Solar System

The solar system consists of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, including eight planets, their moons, dwarf planets, and other small celestial bodies. Earth is one of these planets, and it is located in the habitable zone where conditions are just right for life.

Galaxies

Galaxies are massive systems consisting of stars, stellar remnants, interstellar gas, dust, and dark matter. Our galaxy, the Milky Way, is a spiral galaxy that contains billions of stars. Spiral galaxies are characterized by their spiral structures and are among the most common types of galaxies.

The Universe

The universe is the totality of space and time, including all forms of matter, energy, planets, stars, and galaxies. It began with the Big Bang, an event that occurred approximately 13.8 billion years ago. The expansion of the universe continues to this day.
